How the Assessment Works

Please read these instructions carefully

1

Listen to a Story

You will hear a short story about a person's daily activities. Listen carefully and try to remember as many details as you can.

2

🎤 Immediate Recall

Right after listening, you'll be asked to type the story in your own words. Don't worry about being perfect - just recall what you remember.

3

⏰ Short Break

There will be a 3-minute waiting period. Please stay on the page and relax. You may think about anything except the story.

4

🎤 Delayed Recall

After the break, you'll be asked to type the story again from memory. Try to recall as much as you can.

💡 Tips for Success

  • • Find a quiet place with no distractions
  • • Make sure you can type comfortably
  • • Take your time and think clearly
  • • Don't worry about minor mistakes - just do your best
